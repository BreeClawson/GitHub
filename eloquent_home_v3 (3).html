Why This Work Matters to Me

I Didn't Start Here. I Started in the Trenches.

Before Eloquent Digital Marketing, I ran Breeazy Love Shop — an online boutique specializing in trendy, affordable, and high-quality hair accessories, styling tools, and hair care products. I wore a lot of headbands. I have alopecia, and there was a period where headbands were everything for me. That personal need became a business. And that business became my education.

I had real wins — especially during Covid. Pop-up shops, live sales, an influencer program, online selling. I was moving. But I wasn't running a business. I was running activity. And when things got hard post-Covid — shipping nightmares, pricing chaos, vendor issues — I didn't have the foundation to absorb it.

That experience is exactly what I bring to every client. I've been in it. I understand what it feels like to be making moves without the systems to sustain them — and I know what it looks like when you finally build the foundation that changes everything.

The Journey

An online boutique specializing in trendy, affordable, and high-quality hair accessories, styling tools, and hair care products. Built through pop-up shops, live sales, e-commerce, and an influencer program — this business was my real education in entrepreneurship.

The Women Empowerment Circle Group is a local Michigan-based nonprofit dedicated to uplifting women's mental and emotional well-being. They provide a safe space for community connection, wellness, and self-care experiences — hosting events, book clubs, and meetups throughout the Metro Detroit area.
2022–2023
The Pivot to Marketing
Realized other business owners had great products but no digital presence. Started helping women-owned businesses — then expanded to all established entrepreneurs who needed real support.
2024–Present
Eloquent Digital Marketing LLC
Rebranded to serve all established businesses, creatives, and organizations. Shifted from social media management to digital operations — because infrastructure is what actually moves the needle.
Right Now
Building Out Loud
Documenting the next chapter of Eloquent Digital in real time on LinkedIn. Every strategy decision, every pivot, every lesson. Because this generation of entrepreneurs deserves to see the real journey.
